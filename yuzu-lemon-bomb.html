Yuzu Lemon Bomb

Jump to Recipe

5 minutes

10 saves

4.5 stars

Table of Contents

  3. Steps to make a Yuzu Lemon Bomb

Materials used

  • Cup
  • Spoon

Ingredients

  • Yuzu lemon honey
  • Lime soda or sparkling water
  • Any orange colored juice (I used Tropicana island punch)
  • Lime (optional)

How to make Yuzu Lemon Bomb?

Step 1: Scoop a spoonful of yuzu lemon honey into your cup.

Yuzu contains a great deal of Vitamin C, much more than any lemon or orange. It's also loaded with other minerals and antioxidants, with anti-inflammatory and anti-microbial properties. Honey is known to treat sore throat and cough and strengthen our body's immune system.

Step 2: Add ice.

If you want this drink to be cold, add a few cubes of ice.

Step 3: Add sparkling water or soda.

I used soda this time around, but sparkling water may be better as the yuzu honey is already sweet enough.

Step 4: Add your orange drink of choice.

I choose to use Tropicana's island punch. The flavor will change depending on the type of juice you use.

Step 6: Add a lime and voila!

The lime is entirely step is entirely optional. I just wanted to make it look more fancy. Enjoy the drink!
